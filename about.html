About Changzhou Terui Electronic

Two decades of engineering excellence — from traditional fuse manufacturing to new energy vehicle innovation.

Our Story

Pioneering EV Components Since 2004

Changzhou Terui Electronic Co., Ltd was established in 2004, beginning as a specialized manufacturer of automotive electrical fuses. Over two decades, we've built a reputation for precision manufacturing and uncompromising quality across all provinces in China and more than 30 countries worldwide.

With the global shift toward electric mobility, we established a dedicated New Energy Vehicle Division to design and manufacture high-performance EV charging connectors, sockets, guns, high-voltage wire harnesses, and precision terminal components. Our products comply with GB/T, SAE J1772, and CCS2 IEC62196 international standards.

Backed by German-imported advanced technology, an independent mold R&D center, and a team of expert engineers, we deliver products that meet the highest safety and performance benchmarks — earning FCC, CE, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications.
