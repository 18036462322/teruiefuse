FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For standard products, our MOQ starts from 100 pieces. Sample orders of 1–10 pieces are available for evaluation purposes. Custom OEM/ODM projects may have different MOQ requirements depending on complexity.

What is the typical lead time?

Standard products ship within 7–15 business days. Custom orders typically require 15–30 days depending on specifications. Rush orders can be accommodated — please discuss with our sales team.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes, we provide full OEM and ODM services including custom cable lengths, connector configurations, branding, packaging, and completely new product designs. Our engineering team supports you from prototype to mass production.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for smaller orders. Standard terms are 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Can you ship to my country?

We export to 30+ countries worldwide via sea freight, air freight, and express courier (DHL, FedEx, UPS). We can ship FOB, CIF, or door-to-door based on your preference.
